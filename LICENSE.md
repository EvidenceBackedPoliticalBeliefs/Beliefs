# Creative Commons Attribution–ShareAlike (CC BY-SA)

## Summary

**CC BY-SA** is a Creative Commons license that allows others to **copy, distribute, display, perform, and adapt** the material **for any purpose, including commercially**, **as long as** they give proper credit **and license new creations under the same terms**.

## You Are Free To

- **Share** — copy and redistribute the material in any medium or format  
- **Adapt** — remix, transform, and build upon the material  
- **Commercial use** — allowed

---

## Under the Following Terms

- **Attribution (BY)**  
  You must give appropriate credit, provide a link to the license, and indicate if changes were made.

- **ShareAlike (SA)**  
  If you remix, transform, or build upon the material, you must distribute your contributions **under the same license** as the original.
